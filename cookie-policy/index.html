Cookie Policy

Cookie Settings
The Premier Research Group Ltd and subsidiary companies website uses cookies — a small piece of data sent from the website and stored in the web browser of the visitor’s computer and/or device. Each time the visitor loads the website, the browser sends the cookie back to the server to notify the website of the visitor’s previous activity. This website’s performance cookie (Google.com used for Google Analytics) is not connected to personal information; it is used in aggregate with other website visitors’ data in order to generate statistical reports on how people are navigating and using the website. We intend to use the reports to help us improve the website usage and performance.

Third Party Cookies

Visitors to Premier-Research.com may additionally receive cookies from third party sources such as Twitter (used to display our recent tweets) and Vimeo (used for our embedded webinars). Please visit these third party websites to learn more about their privacy and cookie policies.
