Become a Clinical Trial Investigator

Complete the clinical trial investigator registration form below to express an interest in becoming an investigator with Premier Research.

While we cover all phases of product development and a wide range of therapeutic areas, we place particular emphasis on pediatric research, rare diseases, analgesia, targeted therapeutics, late phase studies and medical devices.

Premier is conducting clinical research in more than 84 countries around the world. Our business is growing and we are always interested in talking to physicians about opportunities to participate in our clinical trials. The first step to getting involved is to complete our registration form. Then a member of our investigator management team will contact you for additional details and will set you up on our clinical investigator database ready for matching with suitable projects.
